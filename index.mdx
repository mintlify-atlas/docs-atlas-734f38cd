## Why ERPNext?

<Note>
ERPNext is built on the Frappe Framework and is used by thousands of companies worldwide to manage their operations efficiently.
</Note>

- **100% Open Source** - Full access to source code under GPL-3.0 license
- **Comprehensive** - All business functions in one integrated system
- **Customizable** - Extend and modify to fit your specific needs
- **Multi-currency** - Support for international operations
- **Cloud or Self-hosted** - Deploy anywhere you want
